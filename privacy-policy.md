# Snapfit Privacy policy

## At SnapFit, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your personal information when you use our application and services.

### Data Collection:
- Account information (username, email address, profile picture)
- Authentication data (securely hashed passwords or third-party login identifiers)
- Training posts, photos, and activity submissions uploaded by users
- Challenge participation data, streaks, points, and performance metrics
- Social connections and interactions (friends, likes, comments, leaderboards)
- App usage data (feature usage, timestamps, interactions, errors)
- Device and technical information (device type, operating system, app version)
- Notification preferences, language settings, and user configuration options

### Data Usage:
- Your data is used to provide, operate, maintain, and improve SnapFit services
- Training photos and posts are stored securely and displayed based on your privacy settings
- Your data helps personalize your experience, including challenges, reminders, insights, and rankings
- We use data to maintain fair play, streak tracking, leaderboards, and social features
- Aggregated and anonymized data may be used for analytics and service improvement
- We never sell your personal information to third parties

### AI & Automated Processing:
- SnapFit uses automated systems to analyze uploaded images to determine whether they relate to sport or fitness activity
- Image analysis may be performed using AI models, including gpt-4.1-nano or equivalent technologies
- Uploaded images may be temporarily processed by third-party AI service providers solely for validation, moderation, and decision-making purposes
- AI-based analysis supports platform integrity, challenge fairness, and abuse prevention
- No automated decision produces legal, medical, or financial consequences for users

### Payments & Monetization:
- SnapFit may offer paid features, subscriptions, or in-app purchases processed through Apple In-App Purchases
- Payment information is processed directly by Apple and is not stored or accessible by SnapFit
- SnapFit receives limited transaction-related information necessary to manage subscriptions and entitlements
- SnapFit may display advertisements using Google Mobile Ads
- Google Mobile Ads may collect device identifiers and usage data in accordance with Google’s privacy policies
- Advertising data is used to deliver relevant ads and measure ad performance

### Data Storage & Retention:
- User-uploaded images are securely stored using cloud storage infrastructure such as Google Cloud Storage
- User account data and activity information are stored in secure databases
- Data is retained only for as long as necessary to provide SnapFit services or comply with legal obligations
- When you delete your account, all associated personal data is permanently deleted unless legally required otherwise
- Backup data is encrypted, access-restricted, and automatically removed within a reasonable timeframe

### Data Sharing:
- We share data only with trusted service providers required to operate SnapFit services
- All third-party providers are bound by confidentiality, security, and data protection obligations
- Data may be disclosed if required by law, legal process, or to protect the rights and safety of users and SnapFit

### Data Security:
- All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms and never stored in plain text
- Access to personal data is limited to authorized systems and personnel
- Technical and organizational measures are used to prevent unauthorized access, loss, or misuse

### Your Rights:
- You can access, review, and manage your data at any time through your profile
- You can update or correct your personal information
- You can delete your account and request permanent deletion of associated data
- You control who can view your profile, posts, and activity
- You can opt out of leaderboard visibility and non-essential notifications
- You may request a copy of your data or request restriction of processing where applicable

### Children’s Privacy:
- SnapFit is not intended for children under the minimum legal age required by applicable law
- We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors
- If such data is identified, it will be deleted promptly

### Policy Updates:
- This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect changes in services, technology, or legal requirements
- Significant changes will be communicated through the app or via email
- Continued use of SnapFit constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

### Contact:
- If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding privacy or data protection, please contact our support team through the app
