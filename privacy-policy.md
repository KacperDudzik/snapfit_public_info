# Snapfit Privacy policy

## At SnapFit, we are committed to protecting your privacy while ensuring a fair, transparent, and rule-based competitive environment. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your personal information when you use our application, including participation in public and private challenges governed by predefined and binding rules.

### Data Collection:
- Account information (username, email address, profile picture)
- Authentication data (securely hashed passwords or third-party login identifiers)
- Training posts, photos, screenshots, and activity submissions uploaded by users\nChallenge participation data (streaks, points, rankings, timestamps of submissions, enrollment times, challenge history)
- Social connections and interactions (friends, likes, comments, leaderboards, rankings)
- App usage data (feature usage, timestamps, interactions, errors, logs)
- Device and technical information (device type, operating system, app version, identifiers)
- Notification preferences, language settings, and user configuration options

### Data Usage:
- Your data is used to provide, operate, maintain, and improve SnapFit services
- Your data is used to enable participation in public and private challenges with fixed rules, defined durations, and predetermined reward structures
- Challenge rankings, scores, and outcomes are calculated automatically using deterministic and rule-based logic (e.g., total completed trainings, timestamps, enrollment order), without manual intervention
- Your data is used to enforce participation limits (e.g., maximum number of concurrent private challenges)\nYour data is used to ensure fair play, detect fraud, prevent abuse, and enforce platform rules\nTraining photos and posts are stored securely and displayed based on your privacy settings
- Your data helps personalize your experience, including challenges, reminders, insights, and rankings
- Aggregated and anonymized data may be used for analytics, performance monitoring, and service improvement\nWe never sell your personal information to third parties

### AI & Automated Processing:
- SnapFit uses automated systems to analyze uploaded images and content for moderation, validation, and challenge participation requirements
- Image moderation may be performed using AI models, including OpenAI GPT-4.1 nano or equivalent technologies, to detect illegal, harmful, unsafe, or inappropriate content
- Screenshots and task-related submissions may be analyzed using AI systems to validate completion of required activities
- Uploaded content may be temporarily processed by trusted third-party AI providers solely for validation, moderation, fraud detection, and decision-making purposes
- Challenge scoring, rankings, tie-breakers, and winner determination are performed fully automatically using predefined algorithms and are final, binding, and non-negotiable
- AI-based analysis supports platform integrity, abuse prevention, fraud detection, and enforcement of content authenticity requirements
- Content that is misleading, irrelevant, manipulated, unsafe, or violates platform rules may be removed
- Users may be restricted, disqualified, or removed from challenges in cases of rule violations or abuse
- No automated decision produces legal, medical, or financial consequences beyond platform-related outcomes

### Payments & Monetization:
- SnapFit may offer paid features, subscriptions, or in-app purchases processed through Apple In-App Purchases
- Payment information is processed directly by Apple and is not stored or accessible by SnapFit
- SnapFit receives limited transaction-related information necessary to manage subscriptions and entitlements
- Public challenges may include monetary rewards distributed according to predefined and deterministic algorithms based on ranking positions\nPrize distribution follows fixed mathematical models and is non-adjustable\nPayments may be processed via Stripe
- SnapFit does not store sensitive financial data (e.g., bank details), which are handled securely by Stripe
- Users are responsible for providing accurate payout information, completing identity verification where required, and complying with applicable tax laws\nPrivate challenges do not include monetary rewards and provide only virtual, symbolic, or in-app recognition
- SnapFit may display advertisements using Google Mobile Ads
- Google Mobile Ads may collect device identifiers and usage data in accordance with Google’s privacy policies\nAdvertising data is used to deliver relevant ads and measure ad performance

### Data Storage & Retention:
- User-uploaded images and content are securely stored using cloud infrastructure such as Google Cloud Storage
- User account data, challenge data, and activity information are stored in secure databases\nData is retained only for as long as necessary to provide SnapFit services, operate challenges, or comply with legal obligations
- Challenge parameters are fixed once a challenge starts and cannot be modified\nIf a user leaves a challenge, all associated progress, points, and challenge data may be permanently lost and cannot be restored
- When you delete your account, all associated personal data is permanently deleted unless legally required otherwise\nBackup data is encrypted, access-restricted, and automatically removed within a reasonable timeframe

### Data Sharing:
- We share data only with trusted service providers required to operate SnapFit services (e.g., cloud infrastructure, AI processing, payment processors)\nAll third-party providers are bound by confidentiality, security, and data protection obligations
- Data may be shared with partners involved in sponsored challenges where necessary to operate those challenges
- Data may be disclosed if required by law, legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, integrity, and security of users and SnapFit

### Data Security:
- All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms and never stored in plain text
- Access to personal data is limited to authorized systems and personnel
- Technical and organizational measures are used to prevent unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or manipulation of challenge systems

### User Responsibilities & Fair Use:
- Users must submit authentic, truthful, and relevant activity content
- Users must not attempt to manipulate scoring systems, rankings, or challenge outcomes
- All participation must comply with challenge rules and platform standards
- Violations may result in content removal, disqualification, loss of progress or rewards, or account restrictions
- All decisions related to rankings, moderation, and disqualification are final and non-negotiable

### Your Rights:
- You can access, review, and manage your data at any time through your profile
- You can update or correct your personal information
- You can delete your account and request permanent deletion of associated data
- You control who can view your profile, posts, and activity
- You can opt out of leaderboard visibility and non-essential notifications
- You may request a copy of your data or request restriction of processing where applicable

### Children’s Privacy:
- SnapFit is not intended for individuals under the minimum legal age required by applicable law (typically 18 years old for participation in competitive challenges)\nWe do not knowingly collect personal data from minors
- If such data is identified, it will be deleted promptly

### Liability Limitation:
- SnapFit is not responsible for loss of challenge progress due to user actions (e.g., leaving a challenge)\nSnapFit is not responsible for technical interruptions, delays, or service outages
- SnapFit is not responsible for third-party service failures (including payment processors)
- SnapFit is not responsible for user-generated content\n\nPolicy Updates:
- This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect changes in services, challenge rules, technology, or legal requirements
- Updates apply to future use and do not retroactively affect active challenges
- Significant changes will be communicated through the app or via email
- Continued use of SnapFit constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

### Contact:
- If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding privacy or data protection, please contact our support team through the app
